Our Story

Chair & Sofa Repair was founded in 2005 by master craftsman John Smith, who had a vision to provide high-quality furniture repair and upholstery services to the local community.

What started as a small workshop has grown into a trusted business with a team of skilled craftspeople dedicated to breathing new life into cherished furniture pieces.

Over the years, we've restored thousands of chairs, sofas, and other furniture items, combining traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques to deliver exceptional results.
